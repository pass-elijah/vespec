## Overhead 1
The checklist portions are pass/fail/na.

- Clock Number
- Inspection Date
- Turn (Probably better to use time)
- Area
- Safety Checklist
   - Limit switches
   - Cable condition
   - Brakes
   - Cables in sheaves
   - Siren
   - Magnet safety lights
- Repair Checklist
   - Loose Material
   - Lights
   - Obvious Damage
   - Fire extinguishers
   - Walkways
   - Radio System
   - Lubrication
   - Guards
   - Housekeeping
   - Windows
   - Railings
